Machine learning Methods in Drug Discovery

Introduction

Drug discovery is a highly complicated process. On average, it takes 6 to 12 years to manufacture a drug and have the product released in the market. Even after a huge investment of money, time and hard work, one can not assure the success of the drug after its release. Drug discovery and design is the process of searching and identifying drugs. The discovery of new drugs is an extremely long and tedious process, it is based on different approaches and strategies. The drug discovery process generally starts by selecting a certain disease and define the list of its unmet medical need, then it moves towards selecting and validating a target molecule considered as druggable by developing both in-vivo and in-vitro assays. Thousands of molecules must be processed and selected, to reach a very limited number of candidates. The number of ligands which have to be tested has increased considerably, we are now talking about 10^13 ligands and still counting, scientists in the pharmaceutical field have no choice to do the work but using computational techniques like Virtual Screening (Vs). The main goal of this process is to predict whether a set of ligands bind to a given protein receptor or not. If yes, scientists can know how strongly. After that ligands are ranked, and only the top ranked ones go to the laboratory.

Motivation

Artificial intelligence(AI) has recently had a profound impact on areas such as image and speech recognition and this progress has already translated into practical applications. Machine Learning is a field of computer science which focuses on creating computational algorithms that can learn complex patterns for a given set of inputs with their corresponding labels and predict and classify the new samples into any of the existing labels. The input data is referred to as training data and the new sample set is the test data. We use this data to train a model for classifying the new data into a predefined class label. The ultimate goal of the various computational methods is to classify and to predict the behavior of test data using the train data with higher accuracy. Applied to the problem of the development of new drugs, machine learning methods have been used in the area of drug discovery since 1962. Machine learning is used in protein structure prediction, protein function prediction and drug target interaction prediction.
