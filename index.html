About Me

I am a passionate PhD Researcher at the University of Skikda 20 Aout 1955, working under the supervision of Prof. BOURAMOUL Abdelkrim, a distinguished professor at the NTIC Faculty, Constantine2 University. My research journey focuses on the innovative application of Artificial Intelligence in drug discovery and development, where I combine advanced machine learning techniques with bioinformatics to revolutionize pharmaceutical research. As a Lecturer Assistant at the University of Skikda and a freelance Data Scientist, I bring practical experience in both academic and industry settings. My work spans across machine learning, deep learning, and bioinformatics, with a particular focus on developing novel computational methods for drug development.
